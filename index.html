About sutuaa


Sutuaa Cleaning Services is a highly equipped startup that specializes in providing professional cleaning services to institutions and individuals. With a dedicated team of skilled cleaners and state-of-the-art cleaning equipment, we are committed to delivering exceptional cleaning solutions that ensure cleanliness, hygiene, and a sparkling environment for our valued clients.


"Sutuaa" provides its services in all governorates of the Sultanate of Oman. At Sutooh, we prioritize quality and excellence in every aspect of our work. SERVICES

At Sutuaa Cleaning Services, we offer a wide range of cleaning services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Our services include:


Residential cleaning

We provide comprehensive residential cleaning services that cover regular cleaning, deep cleaning, move-in/move-out cleaning, and post-construction cleaning. Our skilled cleaners ensure that every nook and cranny of your home is thoroughly cleaned, leaving it spotless and fresh.

Commercial cleaning

We offer customized commercial cleaning solutions to various institutions, including offices, schools, healthcare facilities, retail stores, and more. Our team is trained to handle the specific cleaning requirements of different industries, ensuring a clean and sanitized environment for employees, visitors, and customers.

Carpet and Upholstery Cleaning

Our advanced cleaning techniques and equipment allow us to effectively clean and revitalize carpets and upholstery, removing dirt, stains, and odors. We help our clients maintain the cleanliness and longevity of their carpets and furniture.



Window cleaning

SparkleClean Cleaning Services provides professional window cleaning services for both residential and commercial properties. Our team is equipped to handle windows of all sizes and heights, ensuring streak-free and crystal-clear windows that enhance the appearance of your space.

Nanotechnology Protection

With cutting-edge advancements in nanotechnology, we have taken cleaning and protection to a whole new level. Our service employs tiny, engineered particles that form a protective shield on various surfaces, creating a barrier against dirt, stains, and even harmful microbes. Experience the power of nanotechnology with our unmatched protection service, providing you with peace of mind and a truly elevated cleaning experience.

Pest control

We are committed to providing effective solutions to eliminate harmful pests and rodents that threaten your homes and establishments. We rely on a team of highly trained experts who analyze and assess the problem, offering innovative and suitable solutions. We utilize the latest environmentally safe technologies and pesticides to effectively and permanently eliminate pests and rodents.
