About sutuaa


"Sutuaa" is a promising Omani company specializing in all aspects of cleaning various spaces. With a competent team and advanced cleaning technology, we transform your institution or home into a clean and organized space.


"Sutuaa" provides its services in all governorates of the Sultanate of Oman. At Sutooh, we prioritize quality and excellence in every aspect of our work. Our team consists of highly trained and experienced cleaning workers who dedicate their efforts to providing cleaning services at the highest level. We also utilize the latest cleaning equipment, tools, and environmentally friendly cleaning products to ensure effective and comprehensive cleaning while minimizing environmental impact.

SERVICES

What we offer


Residential cleaning

We provide comprehensive residential cleaning services that cover regular cleaning, deep cleaning, and post-construction cleaning. Our operational staff ensures thorough cleaning of every corner and nook in your home, leaving it completely clean and shining.

Commercial cleaning

We offer customized commercial cleaning solutions for various institutions, including offices, schools, healthcare facilities, retail stores, and more. Our team is trained to handle specific cleaning requirements for different industries, ensuring a clean and sanitized environment for employees, visitors, and customers.

Assest & furniture cleaning

Our advanced cleaning techniques and equipment allow us to effectively clean carpets and upholstery, removing dirt, stains, and unpleasant odors. We help our customers maintain the cleanliness and longevity of their carpets and furniture.



Window cleaning

Sutuaa offers professional window cleaning services for both residential and commercial properties. Our team is equipped to handle windows of all sizes and varying heights, ensuring clean and spotless windows that enhance the appearance of your space.

Nanotechnology Protection

With cutting-edge advancements in nanotechnology, we have taken cleaning and protection to a whole new level. Our service employs tiny, engineered particles that form a protective shield on various surfaces, creating a barrier against dirt, stains, and even harmful microbes. Experience the power of nanotechnology with our unmatched protection service, providing you with peace of mind and a truly elevated cleaning experience.

Pest control

We are committed to providing effective solutions to eliminate harmful pests and rodents that threaten your homes and establishments. We rely on a team of highly trained experts who analyze and assess the problem, offering innovative and suitable solutions. We utilize the latest environmentally safe technologies and pesticides to effectively and permanently eliminate pests and rodents.
